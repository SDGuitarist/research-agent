# Research Brief: The Lodge at Torrey Pines — Current State Assessment

## Date Created
February 14, 2026

## Researcher Background
Professional Spanish guitarist who held a 9-year residency performing at The Lodge at Torrey Pines in La Jolla, California. Performed at both A.R. Valentien (signature fine dining) and The Grill. Strategic goal: reconnect with returning GM Bill Gross about restoring live music programming.

## Known Facts (Insider Context)

### Live Music Timeline
- Researcher performed 9-year residency at A.R. Valentien and The Grill
- April 2025: All live musicians replaced by Acoustic Spot Talent (booking agency)
- ~November 2025: Acoustic Spot Talent also cut
- Current status: No live music at the Lodge (as of Feb 2026)

### A.R. Valentien Restaurant
- Closed for renovation: October 13, 2024
- Reopened: May 9, 2025 with revamped menu
- Executive Chef Kelli Crosson (long career at the Lodge) has since departed
- Staff reports indicate restaurant has been underperforming since reopening

### Leadership Upheaval
- Bill Gross: GM for 10+ years, retired October 2024
- Jakub Skyvara: Replaced Gross as GM (~September 2024), fired January 2026 (~15 months tenure)
- Bill Gross: Returned as GM (January 2026)
- Mass management turnover: department heads across security, restaurant, housekeeping, and culinary all replaced or resigned
- Evans Hotels posted permanent GM job listing for the Lodge on LinkedIn (January 2026)
- Separate GM listing posted for Bahia Resort Hotel

### Broader Context
- Evans family matriarch's death appears to have triggered instability and succession uncertainty
- Staff sources describe skeleton crew conditions, low morale, guest complaints about dated facilities
